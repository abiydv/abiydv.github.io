Notes

Stuff to revisit later, or just for quick reference.

Be warned, many of the notes here may be short, concise, incomplete, or even completely wrong. They aren’t always formatted properly, and may contain broken links. If you do spot something amiss, please feel free to comment. I would be grateful for your help!

Each note is tied to at-least one Topic, and a stage - sandbox, incubating, graduated or archived. Yes, I know I have shamelessly adopted the CNCF project stages.

Stage is a good indicator of how exhaustive a particular note is. Each Topic contains multiple, related notes, and is a good place to find connections.
