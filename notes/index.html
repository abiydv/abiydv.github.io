Notes

Many notes here are incomplete, concise or even completely wrong. Most aren’t formatted properly either, and may contain broken links. They are a work-in-progess. If you do spot something that needs correcting, please feel free to comment.

Each note is assigned a Stage and one or more Topics. A Topic can contain multiple, related notes, and is a good place to find further connections.

Stage is a good indicator of how exhaustive the note is, and is one of  sandbox ,  incubating ,  graduated  or  archived
